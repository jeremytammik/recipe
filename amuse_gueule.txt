# hors d'oevres

## dates with walnuts

cut open the date, fill with cream cheese (e.g. philadelphia), adda walnut on top.


## cherry tomatoes in tequila

marinate cherry tomatoes in tequila for 24 hours, serve in tequila with toothpicks and dip in rough sea salt mixed with lemon zest.


## pumpernickel with fresh cheese and blueberries

cover pumpernickel with frischkaese, arrange five blueberries in blueberry jam and two pieces of schnittlauch on top.
