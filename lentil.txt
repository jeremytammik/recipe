black lentils a la helene

fry some onion and garlic
add cinnamon and cloves, possibly some basil and marjoram
add small black puy lentils and water and boil them
optionally add some vegetables at the end, for instance carrot or courgette
optionally add some lemon juice before serving

red lentils a la monique rath

fry some onions and put them away for garnish
fry some spices in oil: curry and cumin
add some carrot, maybe some ginger
add some apple
add red lentils
add water and vegetable stock
simmer
serve with rice, salted yoghurt, and the separately fried onions

red lentil soup

fry onion, ginger and optionally garlic in butter or oil
add curcuma and curry and fry that also
optionally add grated cocoa-nut
add red lentils and fry some more
optionally add cocoanut milk
add water and vegetable stock
optionally add vegetables such as carrots, leek, whatever you like

always cook with lots of garlic and cumin seeds
eat with something spicy, that gives it some flavour
