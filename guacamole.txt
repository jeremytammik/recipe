guacamole

1 ripe tomato
1 spring onion
1 chili

cut finely and mix

1 avocado 

extract flesh and squash with fork

1 spoonful olive oil
1 spoonful lime juice
1-2 teaspoonful fresh coriander leaves
1 teaspoonful cumin (kreuzkuemmel)

mix and season with salt and pepper

serve with tortilla chips
